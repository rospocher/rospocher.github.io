Abstract

BPMN Ontology (BPMN_Spec_v1.1_January_2008) - Version 1.01

Created By Marco Rospocher, Chiara Ghidini, Luciano Serafini

The BPMN Ontology provides a semantic formalisation of the structural components of the Business Process Modelling Notation (BPMN), based on the latest stable BPMN specifications from OMG [BPMN Version 1.1 - January 2008]. The development of the ontology was guided by the description of the complete set of BPMN Element Attributes and Types contained in Annex B of the BPMN specifications.

Icons within the diamond shape will

indicate the type of flow control behavior.

The types of control include:

1. exclusive -- exclusive decision and
